Body Mass Index (BMI) Calculator

About BMI

BMI is a ratio of weight of your body and height. This is only applicable for above 20 years.
Status of BMI
  1. below 18.4=under weight
  2. 18.4-24.9=ideal
  3. above 24.9=over weight
